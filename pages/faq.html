    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I sign in?

    If this is your first time using ConcoHub, please use your university-provided email and sign up here.



    Otherwise, sign in here.

    I cannot see the list of resources available.

    Before you can see the resources, you need to Sign in here.

    Once you're signed in,



    Then, you may click on "Resources" to view the available resources.

    How do I book a room?

    Click on "Resources" in the navigation menu.



    Select your desired room and date.


    Click on the available time slot you wish to book.


    Confirm your booking details and submit.

    How do I manage my bookings?

    To view and manage your bookings, click on "My Bookings" in the navigation menu.



    Here, you can see all your upcoming bookings. You can modify or cancel them as needed.

    I have a resource that I would like to share — how do I add that to the resource list?

    If you are an administrator, you can add new resources via the Admin Dashboard.



    Click on "Admin Dashboard" to access admin features.



    You then can navigate to the "Resources" section to add new resources.

    My student is awaiting for rental approval. How do I approve their request?

    To approve a request, go to the "Admin Dashboard" page



    navigate to the "Bookings" section.



    Find the request you want to approve and click on the desired actions next to it.

    How do I change my user name or password?

    Using the navigation menu, go to "Settings". From there, you can change your user name or password.

    Who do I contact for support?

    For support, please use the Help Center link or contact us at support@concohub.com.
